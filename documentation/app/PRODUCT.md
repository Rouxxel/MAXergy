# Cloover – The Household Energy Transition Planner

## Product Narrative

People do not buy solar panels.

They buy lower monthly costs.

Cloover transforms complex energy upgrades into a simple financial decision.

Instead of asking:

> Do you want solar panels?

We answer:

> How much could you save every month?

## Core Product Promise

- One Household
- One Upgrade Plan
- One Monthly Number

## Key Differentiator

Traditional process:

Solar → Financing → Tariff

Cloover process:

Outcome → Upgrade Bundle

The customer sees a single monthly savings figure generated from:

- Solar production
- Battery arbitrage
- Heat pump efficiency
- EV charging optimization
- Dynamic tariffs
- Financing

## Product Principles

### Outcome First

Always lead with savings.

### Explainability

Every recommendation must be justified.

### Upsell Through Value

Additional upgrades are recommended only if they increase savings.

Example:

Adding a heat pump increases savings from €42/month to €88/month.

### Minimal Input

A user should complete onboarding in less than three minutes.

## Recommendation Logic

Generate:

- Solar
- Solar + Battery
- Solar + Heat Pump
- Solar + Battery + Heat Pump
- Full Bundle

Rank by:

- Monthly Savings

Select the highest-value scenario.

## AI Advisor Responsibilities

Generate:

- Personalized recommendation
- Savings explanation
- Financing explanation
- Upgrade justification
- Proposal-ready text

## Example Output

Recommended Plan:

Solar + Battery + Heat Pump

Monthly Savings:

€87/month

Reason:

Your household currently spends €312/month on electricity, heating, and mobility.

Installing a solar system with battery storage reduces grid dependency while a heat pump replaces expensive oil heating.

After financing costs, your expected monthly spending decreases to €225/month, resulting in €87/month savings.

Once financing is completed, savings increase to approximately €218/month.
